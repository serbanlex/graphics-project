## Design Tips

- **Consistency:** Use a cohesive color scheme throughout
- **Images:** Large, high-quality images should be the focus
- **Text:** Minimal text per slide — you'll explain verbally
- **Transitions:** Simple, professional transitions
- **Fonts:** Clean, readable fonts (e.g., Calibri, Arial, Montserrat)

---

## Presentation Notes

- Practice to stay within 3-5 minutes
- Focus on the visual story
- Highlight creative decisions and tool comparisons
- Be ready to answer questions about:
  - Specific prompts used
  - Challenges with character consistency
  - Why you chose certain tools for specific tasks
